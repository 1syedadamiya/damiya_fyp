Our Services

Recommendation

Our platform reduces the entire effort for hunting the university that is compatible with students' grades and interests by recommending the perfect match for one against just one form of students' details. It encompasses the call for searching the university near to one's neighborhood and within one's financial range.

Academic Info

This platform fulfils the search of main searches within a university portal all at one stop such as degree programs offered with their respective eligibility Criteria, fee structures, merit lists and merit Calculators. It also dispense the look for aspect of location and budget.

One stop shop

All academic information is available at our platform for the intermediate/ O-level students. This site will help students for getting an idea on which university can best suit their interests and latest scores. It also furnishes the need of other relevant academic information that is not even provisioned on the universities' own sites.
